How It Works

1. Submit this form

Tell us pickup address, date, and parcel details.

2. We confirm within 30 mins

A Nebur agent will WhatsApp or call you to confirm the slot and share a price.

3. Rider arrives

Your rider arrives in your selected window. You'll get a heads-up 15 minutes before.

4. Track your parcel

Once collected, track your parcel every step of the way.

Good to Know

Pickups available 7 days a week, including weekends.

Same-day express pickups must be booked before 11am.

Maximum single parcel weight: 30kg. Heavier shipments — contact us for B2B rates.

We do not handle hazardous materials, cash, or perishables.
