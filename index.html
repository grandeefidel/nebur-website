Two Ways to Ship

Choose how it works for you

Book a home collection or drop off at a nearby point — whichever fits your day. Both get your parcel moving fast.

Home Collection

We come to you. Book online or on WhatsApp and a Nebur rider will collect your parcel right from your door — no travel required.

1

Book online or via WhatsApp

Share your pickup and delivery details in under two minutes. We confirm within the hour.

2

Rider collects from your door

Our rider arrives at the agreed time. You hand over the parcel — that's all you need to do.

3

Delivered to the recipient

Your parcel is delivered safely. Both you and your recipient receive confirmation.

Drop-off Point

Prefer to drop it off yourself? Take your parcel to any of our 200+ partner locations across Nigeria and we'll handle everything from there.

1

Find your nearest drop-off point

2

Drop your parcel off

Hand over your parcel at the partner location. Staff are on hand to label and receipt it.

3

We deliver to the recipient

We collect from the drop-off point and deliver to your recipient's address with full tracking.
