What We Deliver

From a single parcel to bulk B2B freight — Nebur has a service built for every shipping need across Nigeria.

All Nebur Services

Home Collection & Delivery

A Nebur rider comes directly to your door to collect your parcel and delivers it to the recipient's address — no travel required on your part. Our flagship door-to-door service covers all 36 Nigerian states and the FCT. Whether it's a gift going to Kano, a product from Aba, or a document needed urgently in Ibadan — we get it there. Standard delivery windows range from next-day to 3 days depending on destination.

All 36 States + FCT Next-day available Door-to-door Parcel tracking included

Drop-off Points

Take your parcel to any Nebur partner drop-off location near you. We handle collection, transit, and delivery from there. Quick, flexible, and available across hundreds of locations in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and more. No need to wait in for a rider — drop it off at a time that suits you and we take care of the rest.

200+ locations Lagos · Abuja · PH · Ibadan · Kano No waiting for a rider Tracking from drop-off

Express Delivery — Same-Day

Book before 12 noon and your parcel is delivered the same evening within Lagos, Abuja, or Port Harcourt. This service is designed for urgent documents, last-minute gifts, business materials, and anything that simply cannot wait until tomorrow. Our dedicated express riders prioritise your delivery above all else.

Same-day delivery Book before noon Lagos · Abuja · PH only Priority handling

Business Logistics (B2B)

Scale your business operations with Nebur's dedicated B2B freight solutions. Whether you need to move inventory between cities, supply retail partners, or coordinate regular bulk shipments, we offer custom-priced contracts, dedicated account managers, and flexible scheduling. Built for SMEs, manufacturers, distributors, and growing brands.

Bulk shipments Custom pricing Dedicated account manager Regular schedule available Nationwide

E-commerce Fulfilment

Focus on selling — we handle everything after the order. Store your inventory in our Lagos or Abuja warehouses, and when orders come in from your Shopify, WooCommerce, or social media store, we pick, pack, and dispatch them automatically. Same-day dispatch for orders received before 2pm. Perfect for Instagram sellers, Jumia merchants, and independent online stores ready to scale.

Warehousing included Same-day dispatch Shopify · WooCommerce · Social Inventory management Returns handling

Cash-on-Delivery (COD)

Nigeria's most trusted way to buy and sell online. Nebur's COD service lets your customers pay cash at the point of delivery — our rider collects it, we verify it, and we transfer it directly to your account the next morning. No more chasing payments, no payment gateway fees. COD removes the biggest barrier to online sales in Nigeria: trust.

Next-morning settlement No gateway fees Verified cash collection Bank transfer to seller All states

Every Nebur delivery is covered by our parcel protection policy. If your parcel is lost or damaged in transit through our fault, we will compensate you based on the declared value of the item. Simply contact our support team within 48 hours of the expected delivery date with your tracking number and we'll resolve it — no long forms, no unnecessary delays.

When your buyer pays our rider at the door, that payment is logged against your account immediately.
